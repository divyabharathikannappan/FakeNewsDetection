Jennifer Lopez and Kate Moss, two of the most iconic celebrities in the entertainment industry, have been making headlines once again, this time for their unique take on beauty standards. In an exclusive interview with our publication, the two superstars opened up about their experiences with shaving and waxing, and how they think it's time for a change.

According to J. Lo, who has been a long-time advocate for self-acceptance, shaving and waxing are just two of the many beauty standards that women are held to, and it's time to rethink the way we approach beauty. "I think it's every woman's right to choose how she wants to present herself, whether that's through shaving, waxing, or embracing her natural hair," she said.

Kate Moss, who has been known for her iconic pixie cut, echoed J. Lo's sentiments. "I think it's something that's been perpetuated by society, and we need to think about why we're doing it. Is it because we're trying to fit into someone else's idea of beauty, or is it because we truly believe it's the best way to take care of our bodies?"

The two celebrities also touched on the topic of eyebrow maintenance, with J. Lo revealing that she's been embracing her natural brow shape for the past few years. "I used to be so worried about having perfectly shaped eyebrows, but now I think it's so much more liberating to just let them be," she said.

Kate Moss, on the other hand, has been a long-time fan of threading her eyebrows. "I think it's a great way to keep my brows in shape, and it's also a great way to define my features," she said.

Both celebrities also shared their thoughts on the importance of self-care, particularly in the month of February, which is often recognized as Self-Care Awareness Month. "I think it's so important to take time for ourselves and focus on our own well-being, especially during the winter months when we're more likely to feel cooped up and sluggish," said J. Lo.

Kate Moss added, "I think it's something that we should be doing every day, not just in February. We should be taking care of our bodies, our minds, and our spirits every single day."

As for their personal beauty routines, both celebrities shared that they prioritize using natural products and taking care of their skin. "I think it's so important to use products that are healthy for you".
